Executive Summary · Internal Discussion

Lexus must convert its hybrid-era conquest lead into a two-tier BEV portfolio before the 2030 all-electric deadline closes the window.

Europe has tipped to electrified, yet Lexus's parent pool runs the lowest BEV share of any major (12%). Its conquest engine — the LBX, the #2 Lexus model in Europe — is hybrid-only. A serene-premium and an electric-conquest white space sit open. The recommendation: act on both, sequenced, on the next-gen platform.

04Concept Planning

The analysis converges on a two-model BEV portfolio: 'Serene' to own the premium white space, 'LZ' to defend the conquest funnel.

PORTFOLIO LOGICOne platform, two roles. 'LZ' carries volume and renews the conquest funnel as the LBX's hybrid entry point disappears; 'Serene' captures margin and re-establishes brand leadership at the top. Shared architecture de-risks the investment.
